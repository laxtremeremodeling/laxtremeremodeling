Roofs

The style of your roof should reflect the style of your home and your personal style. We spend hours meticulously planning the finest details, accents, and materials to enhance your home’s exterior appeal.

Compliment your home with a roofing renovation that will increase sale value, street appeal and keep you dry. Replaced tiles, a new style or a general repair can greatly increase your home’s appearance and even save you money on cooling and heating bills.

Roofs that creak, leak or have damaged tiles or other roofing materials, will instantly degrade the look of your home and make it less appealing to buyers. At L.A. Xtreme Remodeling we not only repair these issues but entirely re-design the style of your roof.

Compliment your neighborhood and your home’s unique style with a customised roofing style that is weatherproof, modern and long-lasting.
